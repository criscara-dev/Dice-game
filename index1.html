HOw to play

  • Press New Game to start;
  • The game has 2 players, playing in rounds;
  • In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he whishes. Each result get added to his CURRENT score:
  • If the player rolls a 1, all his ROUND score gets lost and it's the next player's turn;
  • The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his GLOBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn;
  • The first player to reach ... 30 points on GLOBAL score, wins the game.
